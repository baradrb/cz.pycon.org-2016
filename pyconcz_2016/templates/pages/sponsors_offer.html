Sponsorship

The PyCon CZ 2016 Community Organizing Committee is pleased to invite your sponsorship and support of the second annual 3 day PyCon event in Brno, Czech Republic on October 28-30th, 2016! Sponsorship and a recruitment booth present opportunities to connect directly with the attending talents.

Last year, the first ever PyCon CZ was held at Fakulta informačních technologií VUT v Brně (FIT) and while small, it was quite the success, and included keynotes from two Python community leaders, Django core developer Russell-Keith Magee and Twisted core developer Amber Brown. Showing the incredible strength of the Python community in Czech Republic respectively. We sold out of our conservatively estimated 250 conference passes in record time!

This year we will again be hosting the event at FIT, but we’re aiming to accommodate 400+ attendees for 2 days of presentations across 3 tracks, 2 lightning talk sessions and a day of sprints and workshops! We’ll also double the number of high profile keynote speakers!

Watch our official PyCon CZ Twitter, Facebook and Google+ feeds for official announcements of important information about the opening of the CfP and more information about our incredible lineup of Keynote speakers! And don’t forget to help us spread the word by following and sharing our messages along with our organizer’s and other passionate Pythonista followers!
